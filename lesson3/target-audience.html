Description of the target

According to the main purpose of this project, we will have several target audiences for our website. Just to make mention, the target audience will include families, businessmen, engineers, young people, fathers, mothers, foreign people, etc. There are many people in this country who are unknown where to go on vacation or enjoy visiting relaxing places. Many of these people fall into traps (fakes) or even worst, they waste valuable time searching for some tourist place. Through this website, we will offer real and trusted information to all people who are looking for a general or specific type of place to visit. All of this information will include prices, offers, tourist packages, staff contact, etc.

Personas

Families: A family is a group of two or more persons related by birth, marriage, or adoption who live together. It is common that families travel o go on vacations many times. So, this website will be focused on this main type of personas.

Retired persons: A retired person is a person who has retired from a working or professional career. This types of persona enjoy traveling or going on vacation many time. They have the recourses and the time to have a great tourist experience. So, this is another main type of personas.
