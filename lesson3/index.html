Purpose of this Site

The main purpose of this website is to offer information about all the tourist places we have in our city. This information includes historical places, museums, hotels, volcanoes, and beaches. This website will have a list of all these places, including addresses, prices, type of service they offer, and a plan to visit all these places when our visitants want. Also, through this website, we will take advantage of the opportunity to offer on-sale products. tourist packages and discounts.
